Scriptable Object Text

Outside Castle Buttercup:

Outside Castle Buttercup

Medieval times

You, the great warrior, Bernard, stand before the Castle Buttercup.  You have come here to rescue Princess Luscious who is locked away in the castle tower.  She was kidnapped by the evil King Buttercup – some grievance over being teased when he was a child in school.  Hey, his last name is BUTTERCUP.  You were not going to just let that slide! 

King Buttercup and his guards are no match against the strength and agility of a great warrior such as yourself.  Actually, you are quite near sighted and have no eye / hand coordination whatsoever.  But, you are not about to let that keep you from saving your beloved, your honey bunch, your main squeeze, your love bunny…you get the idea.  She is REALLY curvy too!

The castle is surrounded by a moat.  A drawbridge stands before you leading across the moat to the castle entrance. 

What do you, the great warrior, Bernard, do next?

Press 1 to employ stealth and swim across the moat

Press 2 to boldly charge across the drawbridge

Moat:

Moat

As you reach the bank of the moat, you look down to see the waters are infested with alligators.

Yikes!  Looks like a strategic retreat is in order.

Did they say “yikes!” in medieval times? Whatever.

Press 1 to retreat

Drawbridge:

Drawbridge

As soon as you step onto the drawbridge, dozens of horrific creatures with menacing, grotesque claws, large, razor sharp teeth and wings to give them flight appear before you.  Normally, this would be a show stopper.  However, you know these creatures are the creation of the wicked witch Gertrude who is in league with the evil King Buttercup.  You also know all of wicked witch Gertrude’s creatures melt away when exposed to water.  And, there just happens to be five gallon buckets of water lining each side of the drawbridge at five foot intervals from one end of the drawbridge to the other. 

Seriously, what are the chances of that?  It is almost as if this were a convenient plot device in some fictional tale.  Nah!  That’s crazy talk! 

You march across the drawbridge laying waste to the hideous creatures with buckets of water as you go. You reach the end of the drawbridge.

Press 1 to enter the castle

Castle Grand Entrance:

Castle Grand Entrance

You are in the grand entrance of Castle Buttercup.  To your right is a closed door.  In front of you, there is a long, winding staircase leading up to the castle tower. 

Press 1 to enter the door on your right 

Press 2 to climb the staircase

Side Room:

Side Room

You enter the room to find 15 of King Buttercup’s servants wearing nothing but their skivvies engaged in a ferocious food fight.  Food is flying everywhere! 

Upon your entrance, the food fight comes to a screeching halt and all of the servants turn to stare at you. 

This is awkward. 

You make your excuses and slowly back out of the room closing the door quietly behind you. You can hear the sounds of the food fight continuing on the other side of the closed door.

Wow!.......Just.......Wow!

Did they say “wow!” in medieval times? Moving on.

Press 1 to return to the grand entrance

Door To Castle Tower:

Door to Castle Tower

You climb the staircase.  At top of the stairs, you face a large, very solid looking door.  The door is locked.  To the right of the door, a key hangs on a hook.

Press 1 to break the door down using your brute strength 

Press 2 to unlock the door using the key hanging on the hook

Break Through Castle Tower Door:

Break Through Castle Tower Door

Your hurl yourself at the door many times attempting to break through.  Your left shoulder is now a mass of nasty looking black and blue bruises.  

The door still stands before you with no sign you have had any effect on it. 

The key is looking like a very attractive option right now. 

Press 1 to reconsider your options

Castle Tower:

Castle Tower

You enter the castle tower to find the beautiful and really curvy Luscious standing before you.  Once the shock that you actually made it this far wears off, Luscious runs to you, throws her arms around you and gives you a passionate kiss that goes on and on. 

Yeah! That’s the ticket! 

Luscious and Bernard are together again in true bliss…..for now. 

Will King Buttercup have his revenge? 

Will wicked witch Gertrude figure out how to create hideous creatures that don’t melt when exposed to water? 

Will Luscious one day realise she could do better? 

Don’t miss the next instalment of the Castle Buttercup adventure! 

Press 1 to play again

Press 2 to exit
